# Product Overview

## Product Name
**BrewDesk** *(working title: Coffee Shop Intelligence Platform)*

## One-line Description
An all-in-one coffee-shop operating system that unifies ROI tracking, stock control, employee & barista management, and a cashier POS — wired in real time to a live balance sheet.

---

## Problem Being Solved
Independent and multi-outlet coffee shop owners are forced to juggle disconnected tools — separate POS software, manual spreadsheets, standalone payroll and inventory apps. The result:

- Financial snapshots are always stale (month-end reconciliation).
- Stock drift and waste go undetected until margins are already hurt.
- Labour costs are invisible until the payroll run.
- There is no ingredient-level cost-to-cup ROI — so profitable products and loss-makers look identical.

---

## Solution
A single integrated web application where every POS transaction simultaneously:
1. Decrements the stock ledger (ingredient level).
2. Posts revenue and cost entries to the live balance sheet.
3. Contributes to per-product and shop-wide ROI calculations.
4. Is attributed to the staff member / barista who processed it.

The owner sees real-time P&L, inventory status, labour costs, and ROI — all from one dashboard, with no manual entry.

---

## Core Modules

| Module | Key Capabilities |
|---|---|
| **ROI Tracking** | Real-time return-on-investment per menu item, per day, per outlet; live P&L; product-margin breakdown |
| **Stock Control** | Ingredient-level inventory; auto-decrement on every POS sale; low-stock alerts; purchase order tracking |
| **Employee & Barista Management** | Staff profiles; shift scheduling; attendance & clock-in/out; labour-cost posting to P&L |
| **Cashier POS** | Fast order entry; payment processing; real-time sync to stock and financials; per-cashier session tracking |
| **Live Balance Sheet** | Real-time financial statements; revenue, COGS, labour, and gross-profit updated on every transaction |

---

## Target Users

- **Primary:** Independent coffee shop owners and managers (single or multi-outlet).
- **Secondary:** Baristas and cashiers (front-of-house operators); shop managers overseeing shifts.
- **Scale:** Small-to-medium specialty coffee businesses; applicable to café chains up to ~10 outlets.

---

## Positioning
> "Your entire coffee business, one dashboard."

Built for operators who want financial clarity *now* — not at month-end. Competes against fragmented point solutions (standalone POS, standalone inventory, manual spreadsheets) by being the only platform where the POS, stock, staff, and balance sheet speak the same language in real time.

---

## Brand & Tone
- **Palette:** Warm amber (`#D97706` / amber-600) on white and slate-900 dark backgrounds.
- **Tone:** Confident, practical, and operator-friendly. No jargon. Numbers-first.
- **Tagline:** *"Your Entire Coffee Business, One Dashboard."*
- **Category label:** Coffee Shop Intelligence

---

## Scope Boundaries (MVP)
- Web application (responsive; landscape and portrait layouts supported).
- Single currency; no multi-currency at launch.
- Balance sheet is a live internal ledger (not a GAAP-certified accounting export at MVP).
- No franchise / royalty management in v1.

---

## Strategic Principles
1. **Every action posts to finance.** No transaction is isolated — POS, stock movements, and labour all flow to the balance sheet automatically.
2. **Real-time by default.** Dashboards show live state, not batch snapshots.
3. **Role-appropriate views.** Cashiers see the POS; managers see shift costs; owners see full ROI and P&L.
4. **Simplicity over completeness.** A clean, fast interface beats a feature-bloated one for daily front-of-house use.
