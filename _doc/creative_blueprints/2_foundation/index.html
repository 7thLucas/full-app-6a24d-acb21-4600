Why Coffee Shops Need

The Problem

No live financial picture

Owners reconcile spreadsheets at month-end — by then, margins have already eroded and decisions are made on stale data.

Disconnected operations

POS, inventory, payroll, and purchasing live in separate tools. A single busy weekend can silently drain stock and profits.

Blind ROI on every cup

Without ingredient-level costing tied to sales, it's impossible to know which products actually make money.

✓

The Solution

Real-time balance sheet

Every sale, stock movement, and labour cost posts instantly to a live P&L — no reconciliation lag, no surprises.

One unified platform

POS cashier, stock control, employee management, and ROI tracking all integrated and speaking the same language.

Profitability per product

Track exact ROI per menu item — from bean cost to cup sold — so every pricing decision is backed by live data.
